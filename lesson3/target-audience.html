Target Audience

We are catering our website services to those who are advanced planners as well as those who just want to know what the weather will be like so they can make sure their day is amazing. If you are someone who is worried that your plans might be ruined due to the weather we are here for you. If you are someone who is an event planner and needs to make sure the weather will be what it is supposed to be, we are here for you.

Personas

Ricky is 26 years old and works everyday at a construction site. He always wants to make sure he wears the right clothes to work so he can stay healthy and strong for his family. In the past when he wouldn't check the weather he would wear a long sleeve when he needed a jacket. The result, he would come home sick and wouldn't be able to spend as much time with his family. Ricky needs to know the weather everyday for his job, his family, and his well-being.

Patricia is an advid bycyclist who rides her bike everyday, if she can. She stays in the best shape of her life by riding her bicycle. Some days she has riden her bike and gotten sick because she was riding through rain or snow. Patricia wants to make sure she can ride everyday but with reason. By checking the weather she can make sure she has the best ride of her life while staying as safe and healthy as possible.

Scenarios

  • Can I take my kids for a bike ride today?
  • Will it be a good day to go swimming?
  • Will the weather be good for my wedding day?
  • How much snow are we going to get today?
  • Is it a good day to go on a hike?
  • Will it get hot enough to cook eggs on the asphalt?
