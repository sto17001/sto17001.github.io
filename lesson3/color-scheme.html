Color Scheme

The main colors used in the When will there be Good Weather website were:

  • Steele Blue
  • Yellow Green
  • White
  • Black

Steel Blue was used for the whole background of the website.

Yellow Green was used for most of the headings and the footer.

White and Black were used in some small places throughout the website like text or outlines.

Steele Blue was also used for the main heading, When will there be Good Weather?
