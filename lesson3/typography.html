Typography

The main fonts used in the website were:

  • 'Roboto Condensed', sans-serif-used in the headers and footer.
  • 'Montserrat', sans-serif-used in the main body of the website information.

The main header at the h1 level are sized at 3em. Headings at h2, which are most of the other headings throughout, are sized at 1.75em. While the rest of the body text are sized at 18px.
