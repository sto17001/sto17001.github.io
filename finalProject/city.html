Historical and Current Demographic Information

In 2018, Rexburg, ID had a population of 28k people with a median age of 23.2 and a median household income of $29,910. Between 2017 and 2018 the population of Rexburg, ID grew from 27,369 to 27,957, a 2.15% increase and its median household income grew from $26,341 to $29,910, a 13.5% increase.
