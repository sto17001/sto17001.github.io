Rule of Thirds

Google Ventures

gv.com/portfolio

The Google Venture portfolio section of their website demonstrates a very clean example of the rule of thirds. By breaking up the page in three different sections it helps attract the viewers attention. They also put the most importatnt information in the top left grid, where the viewer is most likely to look.

Alignment

Nordstrom

nordstrom.com

On Nordstrom's website they have used the alignment property perfectly all throughout. They group many similar items together, helping our brains group them together and making it more appealing. The use of the alignment is clean and helps keep things grouped together.

Repetition

Walmart

walmart.com

Walmart has a great repetition of their colors and block formatting on their website. The repetition helps keep things familiar but it isn't annoying about it. It helps guide the viewer in a clear way as well as organize infomration coherently. Overall, I think walmart does a good job at using repetition within their website.
